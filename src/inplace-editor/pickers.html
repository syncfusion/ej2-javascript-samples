This sample demonstrates the usage of picker controls such as Date, Time, DateTime, and DateRange. Click on the dotted input element to switch to the editable state of the corresponding integrated component.

This sample illustrates the way to integrate picker controls with the In-place Editor control. The applicable types of components are:

  • DatePicker
  • TimePicker
  • DateTimePicker
  • DateRangePicker

The above controls and their features are editable in place and can be customized with the model properties of the specific component.
